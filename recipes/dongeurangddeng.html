Korean Dongeurangddeng

Dongeurangddeng is a traditional korean dish that is usually made as a holiday appetizer or side dish, most notably, for Korean New Year! It is essentially a panfried flat meatball packed with delicious tofu, pork, beef, carrots, onions and green onions. They are typically served with ketchup, but can also be served with soy sauce, sriracha or gochujang.

Ingredients

  • 1/2lb Ground beef
  • 1/2lb Ground pork
  • Firm tofu water squeezed out with cloth or paper towel and crushed
  • 1/2 Carrot
  • 1/2 Onion
  • 2 Green onion
  • 1 tbsp soy sause
  • 2 tsp Flour
  • 1/2 tbsp Vegetable oil

Directions

  1. Finely dice onion, carrot and thinly slice green onion
  2. Add ground beef, pork, crushed tofu, cute veggies, soy sauce and flour to a large bowl. Mix thoroughly using hands.
  3. Shape meat mixture into 2 inch balls and press softly to flatten.
  4. Beat eggs in a small bowl
  5. Heat 1/2 tbsp oil in a large pan over medium high heat. Dredge meatballs in egg wash and cook meatballs for about 2 minutes on each side until cooked through.
  6. Remove from heat and serve with sauce of your choice!
